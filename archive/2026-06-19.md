Yesterday's theme was agent discipline — ponytail's YAGNI minimalism, mattpocock/skills, and Agent-Reach social access all landed simultaneously and pointed at how agents should behave. Alibaba's zvec vector DB at +259 and ClaudioDrews/memory-os at 1,168 stars round out a session where the story is no longer what agents can do, but how they store, index, and retrieve context at scale.
