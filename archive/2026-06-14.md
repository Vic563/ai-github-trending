## Editor's Take

Ponytail had the single largest 24-hour gain this tracker has ever recorded for a two-day-old repo: it jumped from 1,083 stars yesterday to 3,082 today (+1,999), outpacing addyosmani/agent-skills (which still gained +1,452 of its own). Compared to yesterday's edition, the agent-skills cohort is no longer the whole story: two distinctly non-agent AI projects broke into the GitHub trending top 5 in terms of daily gains — shiyu-coder/Kronos (+238, a financial foundation model at nearly 30k stars) and Introduction-to-Autonomous-Robots (+276, a textbook now going viral in robotics circles). The observability and security layer around agents (NVIDIA/SkillSpector +804, kenn-io/agentsview +384) continues to consolidate fast, a pattern that began Wednesday and shows no sign of stopping. New entrant to watch: microsoft/intelligent-terminal, a quiet fork of Windows Terminal with native agent integration — no announcement post, just 984 stars in 27 days from a team that ships, not hypes.
