## Editor's Take

openhuman gained +3,941 stars today, up from +1,549 two days ago, making this the third consecutive day the project has ranked in this archive and its fastest single-day acceleration yet. The more notable structural shift is that three established repositories with a combined 437,000+ total stars all appeared on trending simultaneously: obra/superpowers (198K, created Oct 2025), multica-ai/andrej-karpathy-skills (137K, created Jan 2026), and msitarzewski/agency-agents (101K, created Oct 2025), all predating the current agent framework wave but centering on methodology and skills design rather than new tooling. Their co-occurrence suggests a coordinated viral event, likely a social thread driving attention back to foundational practice, and the result is that seven of today's top 20 repos fall into the skills/methodology/educational category, a sharp contrast to May 17's product-launch-heavy composition. Token efficiency is emerging as an independent category: rtk-ai/rtk (60-90% token reduction via CLI proxy) and colbymchenry/codegraph (pre-indexed code knowledge graph to cut Claude Code overhead) both hold top-11 positions, the clearest signal yet that cost-of-inference is becoming a design constraint developers optimize for explicitly. HKUDS is the only research group with two repos in today's top 20, as ViMax (agentic video generation) joins CLI-Anything, extending the lab's footprint from agent-native CLI tooling into multimodal generation.
